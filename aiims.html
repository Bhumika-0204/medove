About AIIMS Delhi

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) New Delhi is a public hospital and medical research university based in New Delhi, India. The institute is governed by the AIIMS Act, 1956 and operates autonomously under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

AIIMS Delhi serves as the flagship institution among all AIIMS institutions across India. It's renowned for its exceptional medical education, cutting-edge research facilities, and comprehensive patient care services. The institute has pioneered several advanced medical procedures and continues to be at the forefront of medical innovation in India.
